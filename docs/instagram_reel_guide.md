# Instagram Reel — App Showcase Guide

Goal: Create a short 3D animated reel with audio to promote the Video Game
Stats Tracker web app on Instagram and attract users and potential partners.

---

## Tools

| Tool | Purpose | Cost |
|---|---|---|
| [Runway ML](https://runwayml.com) | 3D animation / text-to-video | ~$15/month |
| [Pika Labs](https://pika.art) | Image-to-video motion (easier) | Free tier available |
| [Canva AI](https://canva.com) | Slide-style promo with transitions | Free tier available |
| [ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.io) | AI voiceover narration | Free tier (short clips) |
| [CapCut](https://capcut.com) | Final edit, music, captions | Free |

**Recommended stack:** Runway ML + ElevenLabs + CapCut

---

## Workflow

### Step 1 — Capture the App
- Take screenshots of key screens:
  - Stats page (charts, personal bests)
  - QueuePanel (social media toggles)
  - An actual Instagram post the automation generated
  - Bolt AI panel (ask a question, show the response)
- Record a short screen recording (15–30s) walking through the app if possible

### Step 2 — Animate with Runway ML
- Upload screenshots or screen recording to [Runway](https://runwayml.com)
- Use **Image-to-Video** or **Gen-3 Alpha** for cinematic 3D motion
- Prompt suggestions:
  - `"Smooth cinematic zoom into a dark-themed gaming dashboard, glowing stats, neon accents"`
  - `"3D parallax motion on a mobile app screen showing bar charts and gaming stats"`
- Generate 3–5 short clips (4–8 seconds each)

### Step 3 — Write the Script
Keep it under 30 seconds for a reel. Example:

> "You play every day. Now your stats do the work.
> Log your sessions. Track your personal bests.
> Let AI break down your performance.
> And automatically post your highlights — no editing required.
> Video Game Stats Tracker. Play more. Know more."

### Step 4 — Record Voiceover with ElevenLabs
- Go to [ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.io)
- Paste the script, choose a voice (recommended: confident, neutral male or female)
- Export as MP3

### Step 5 — Edit in CapCut
1. Import Runway clips in order
2. Add the ElevenLabs voiceover as audio track
3. Add background music (CapCut has a royalty-free library — pick something energetic)
4. Add text overlays for key features:
   - "Track Any Game"
   - "AI-Powered Insights"
   - "Auto-Post to Instagram & Twitter"
5. Export at 1080×1920 (Instagram Reel format, 9:16)

## Reel Structure (30 seconds)

| Timestamp | Content |
|---|---|
| 0–3s | Hook — app opening screen or a dramatic stat chart |
| 3–10s | Stats page walkthrough — charts, personal bests |
| 10–17s | Bolt AI panel — ask a question, answer appears |
| 17–24s | QueuePanel + actual Instagram post output |
| 24–30s | CTA — "Link in bio" or "Sign up free" |

## Tips
- Keep the reel under 30 seconds for best reach
- First 2 seconds must hook — start with the most visually striking chart
- Subtitles increase watch time significantly (CapCut auto-generates them)
- Post between 6–9pm local time for peak engagement
- Pin the reel to your profile after posting
